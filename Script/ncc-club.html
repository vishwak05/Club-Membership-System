NCC Club

Welcome to the NCC Club!

The NCC Club is focused on instilling discipline, leadership, and patriotism among students. It's a platform for those interested in defense services and serving the nation. Our club conducts various drills, camps, and training sessions aimed at physical and mental development.

Participating in NCC can be a transformative experience, offering opportunities to join adventurous activities, national integration camps, and social service initiatives. Join the NCC Club to develop your leadership skills, enhance your resilience, and contribute to national development.
