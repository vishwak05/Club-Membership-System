NSS Club

Welcome to the NSS Club!

The NSS Club is dedicated to community service, aiming to develop student personality through community service. Our club is a great platform for those who wish to contribute to society and learn about social responsibility.

Our activities include community clean-up drives, teaching underprivileged children, blood donation camps, and various social awareness campaigns. Join us to make a difference in the community and develop your skills in leadership, teamwork, and social engagement.
