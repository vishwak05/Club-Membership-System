Coding Club

Welcome to the Coding Club!

The Coding Club is dedicated to bringing together students who are passionate about coding and technology. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced coder aiming to enhance your skills, our club provides a supportive environment for all.

We organize workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and coding competitions to provide practical experience and learning opportunities. Join us to connect with like-minded peers, develop your coding abilities, and work on exciting projects.
